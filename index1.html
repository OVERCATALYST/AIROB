TERMS AND CONDITIONS

Read the Terms & Conditions carefully before buying your tickets

  • The hackathon is open to teams with a minimum of four and a maximum of five members.
  • Each team is required to have at least one laptop.
  • After the payment, no refunds will be processed.
  • Participants should bring their college ID card/Government-Approved ID Card to enter the premises.
  • A soft copy of the IEEE membership card has to be shown at the registration desk. Non-IEEE members registered as
    IEEE members and Non-CS/RAS members registered as CS/RAS members will be imposed with a fine of Rs. 400.
  • Students registered as IEEE CS/RAS Members should provide their CS/RAS Membership subscription while registering
    and should keep a soft copy to be shown at the registration desk.
  • Your vehicle is not allowed inside the campus as well, and no safeguarding of your vehicle will be provided by the authorities.
  • A cloakroom will be provided inside the venue. However, the organizers won't be held responsible for any lost
    wallets, ornaments, etc.
  • Students shouldn't leave the college premises without providing valid consent to the event coordinators.
  • Beds will be provided. You are requested to bring a bed sheet and the necessary commodities if required.
